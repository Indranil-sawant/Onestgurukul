Admission Requirements

  • Birth Certificate (Municipal/Aadhaar linked)
  • Aadhaar Card (Student + Parents)
  • Address Proof (Electricity bill/Rent agreement)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photos (Student: 4, Parents: 2)
  • Medical Certificate (if applicable)

All original documents must be submitted for verification at the time of admission
